Our Story

25 Years of Uncompromising Quality

Founded in 1999 by a small team of passionate engineers and architects, Apex Construction began with a simple belief: that every structure built deserves the absolute best in craftsmanship, materials, and vision.

Over two decades later, we have grown into one of the northeast's most respected construction firms — having completed over 250 landmark residential and commercial projects, each one a testament to that founding belief.

Our heritage is built on relationships: with our clients, our craftsmen, and the communities where we work. We don't just build buildings. We engineer legacies.
